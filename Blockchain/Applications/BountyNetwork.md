# Bounty Networks

Bounty networks present tasks to users. Users can choose the tasks that they would like to fulfill in exchange for the posted bounty.

* [Gitcoin](https://gitcoin.co/)
* [Bounties.network](https://bounties.network/)
* [Bounty0x](https://bounty0x.io/)
* [Ethereum Bounty Program](https://bounty.ethereum.org/#rules)
* [Fund Request](https://fundrequest.io/)
* [Status Open Bounty](https://openbounty.status.im/)
